Who is Dr Gwel?

I started streaming on Twitch in October 2021. Throughout my broadcasts, I’ve sought to build a community around topics I’m passionate about: gaming and science.

During each live, I aim to create a chill and welcoming atmosphere, constantly striving for quality and ambition in the resources I use. This applies to the hardware (which I acquired thanks to subscribers) and the software, scripts and interactive elements. I take great pleasure in connecting with my community to share my life as a researcher and experience unforgettable moments together.

Collabs and engagement

I joined the Sea of ​​Thieves France team as Chief Editor and Live Content Manager, with the aim of energizing the French-speaking gaming community and producing exceptional events. SOTFR is a group of gaming enthusiasts who are part of the Affiliate Alliance, a group of communities officially recognized by the development studio, Rare.


I also joined the collective Pogscience, with whom I share the goal of producing quality outreach content on Twitch. We bring together diverse expertise and seek to promote scientific content in French, with the ambition of connecting different actors in online popularization with the public.


Finally, I'm part of the Black Sails team, where we highlight and connect Sea of ​​Thieves streamers of Québec.
